Welcome to PVP Siddhartha Institute of Technology

PVP Siddhartha Institute of Technology, established in 1998, is a pioneering institute under the aegis of the Siddhartha Academy of General & Technical Education, Vijayawada. Renowned for academic excellence, the institution offers a wide array of opportunities for holistic development and career success.

Key Highlights

  • NAAC has conferred A+ grade to the institute.
  • All UG Programs are accredited by NBA.
  • ISO 9001-2015 certified for quality and academic excellence.
  • 100% ragging-free environment.
  • Approved by AICTE and permanently affiliated to JNTUK.
  • Autonomous status since 2012, offering a curriculum aligned with industrial requirements.
  • Outcome-based education as the foundation of the learning system.

Student-Centric Initiatives

  • Guidance and support for industrial internships and paid internships.
  • A strong training and placement cell to nurture employability skills.
  • Entrepreneurship support for students to kick-start their ideas.
  • Active NSS and NCC wings to foster social responsibility.
  • Technical and professional societies for learning beyond the classroom.
  • Cultural and hobby clubs for the all-round development of students.

Training & Placements

We provide robust training and placement opportunities to ensure our students achieve their career aspirations. Collaborating with leading companies, we offer 100% placement assistance.

About Us

The Training and Placement Cell of PVP Siddhartha is established with the sole aim of finding suitable employment opportunities for all eligible students. It caters to all the training requirements of students through highly experienced faculty. The unique program of skill enhancement identifies and nurtures the talent in job aspirants and ignites the young minds to capably and confidently face the recruitment drives.

Need-based curriculum that is specially designed fine-tunes the student to the corporate requirements. The placement cell has state-of-the-art infrastructure and a rich ambiance to facilitate the training process. The students of PVP are provided excellent training that enhances their potential and improves their confidence levels. The facilities include a well-furnished Auditorium, GD rooms, closed interview panels, and conference halls.
